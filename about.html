About Me

Hi, I am an astrophysicist, currently pursuing my PhD at the University of Ferrara, jointly enrolled at the University of Bologna through a collaborative project within the FutureData4EU Marie Skłodowska-Curie COFUND Programme. My research lies at the intersection of astrophysics and data science, with a focus on multimessenger transient phenomena. I work on double white dwarf merger remnants and kilonova modelling using statistical and numerical methods. In the era of LSST at the Vera C. Rubin Observatory, these modelling are essential for identifying and interpreting such transient events.

Beyond research, I enjoy doing music and travelling, which help me stay creatively inspired and connected to the world beyond science.
