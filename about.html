About Me

Hi, I am an astrophysicist, currently pursuing my PhD at the University of Ferrara, jointly enrolled at the University of Bologna through a collaborative project within the FutureData4EU Marie Skłodowska-Curie COFUND Programme. My research lies at the intersection of astrophysics and data science, with a focus on multimessenger transient phenomena. I work on double white dwarf merger remnants and kilonova modelling using statistical and numerical methods. These systems are key progenitors of faint, rapidly evolving optical transients. In the era of LSST at the Vera C. Rubin Observatory, such modelling is essential for identifying and interpreting these events.

Beyond research, I enjoy music and travelling, which keep me creatively inspired.
