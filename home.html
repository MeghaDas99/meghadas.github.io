About Me

I am a Marie Skłodowska-Curie PhD Researcher within the FutureData4EU programme. My research focuses on multimessenger transient astrophysics, with particular emphasis on double white dwarf merger remnants and kilonova modelling.

I have expertise in data reduction and statistical modelling, working at the intersection of data science and astrophysics to understand complex transient phenomena in the universe.

Beyond research, I enjoy doing music and travelling, which help me stay creatively inspired and connected to the world beyond science.
